Dear Declarations,


I'm Camilo Garcia, a visual artist, designer and amateur hacker/coder who likes to think and make physical and digital works.


Although I jump between practices,
I draw at heart.
Coding practices have constrained my gestures,
my hands are restricted from molding and very often impulses of crafting possessed me. <br>


However, as a coder,
the text became poetry that

invokes:


midnightblue tinted light,

snow falls on olive lines,

coral dawn whispers.


colors have names and
comments are notes to the self(inspect me).
